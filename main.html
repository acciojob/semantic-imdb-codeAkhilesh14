Swades (2004) is a celebrated Indian drama starring Shah Rukh Khan. It follows Mohan, a successful NASA scientist, who returns to India to find his childhood nanny. During his visit, he witnesses rural realities and decides to help the village develop, ultimately discovering his profound connection to his homeland.
